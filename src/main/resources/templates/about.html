About Sync&Flow

Sync&Flow is a document management and version control system designed to help teams create, review, approve, and organize documents in one clean workflow.

What the system does

The platform keeps document history, allows controlled uploads of new versions, supports review and approval flow, and helps users always work with the correct active version.

  • Document creation with TXT upload
  • Version history and approval workflow
  • Reviewer comments and status tracking
  • Role-based permissions
  • Audit logging of important actions

Why Sync&Flow

Instead of editing files manually and losing track of the latest version, users can manage everything in one place with clear roles, safer updates, and visible version history.
